Optimal Amount of Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2
Meat 1-6 (Recommend 3)
Cheese 1-2
Letture 3
Tomatoes 2
Onions 1-3
Pickles 1-4 (Recommend 3)

Now Put it Together!

Steps!

  1. Place first piece of bread down
  2. Place all meat on slice
  3. Place Tomatoes
  4. Place Onions
  5. Place Pickles
  6. Place Lettuce
  7. Place final piece of bread
